His head was spinning.
<&>「It's already been eight years since I entered this world.」
For those eight years, Wukong repeated the same days every day. He ate what the youngest ate and wore what the youngest wore. Experiencing human life, Wukong wondered what kind of world the youngest dreamed of.
<&>「Is this the 'ordinary life' the youngest sought?」
Wukong couldn't tell. He'd never experienced this kind of 'ordinary life' in the first place.
To him, everyday life was a bloody battlefield. A battle between constellations vying for legends. A coliseum of stars gazing at useful incarnations.
<&>「"But wasn't I twenty-eight last year?"」
A mere eight years. To the stars, it was a fleeting moment. To Wukong, who had been buried beneath the Five Elements Mountain for hundreds of years, it was a short time. But in those eight years, Wukong had learned and mastered quite a lot.
<&>「"But wouldn't it be nice to stay twenty-eight?"」
Now, Wukong could think quite humanly. He understood human desires and was able to live like one.
He learned these things from Hyunsung and Heewon. That you need to earn money to survive. That you shouldn't arbitrarily take others' property. The value of spending time with someone.
As he savored every moment of his mortal life, Wukong finally felt a little closer to the youngest's life. But...
<&>「"What are you talking about? I can't stay twenty-eight forever. The stories I love will just stay there."」
Perhaps the youngest wanted to escape this world he had protected all along.
"I'm asking. Who are you?"
The being who had taken over the youngest's body didn't respond.
He worried it might be Asmodeus or something similar, but he sensed no such aura within the youngest's body.
He sensed a faint magical energy, but it wasn't like the demon kings he knew. It was more like the scent of an old book.
A scent that touched the deep recesses of his mind, a memory box.
Wukong frowned unconsciously and asked.
"Are you possessed?"
Kim Dokja, his shoulders hunched weakly, didn't answer.
"Tell me. How did you break through the prison? How did you take over the youngest's body?"
<&>「There can be no 'possessor' capable of possessing the youngest.」
The youngest is the very foundation of this world. The essence of this world is rooted in the youngest's imagination.
To take over the youngest's body, one must possess imagination that surpasses even the youngest's capacity, or be a being whose soul is essentially identical to the youngest's.
"If you don't answer."
Wukong grabbed Kim Dokja by the collar with a scorching voice.
"I'll kill you."
A faint, burgeoning murderous aura engulfed the surroundings. But Kim Dokja remained silent, his eyes blank. Watching the youngest, Wukong slowly grew angry.
<&>「It was a world we had worked so hard to protect.」
He didn't know who this man was. But if the youngest had been harmed in a way he didn't know, if that was why this world was threatened.
<&>「It was the only worldline where the youngest's dream could come true.」
Wukong, eyes narrowed, grabbed Kim Dokja by the collar and raised his other hand.
"That's it."
A cold blade touched the back of Wukong's neck. Wukong knew the feel of that blade.
"Put Dokja-ssi down."
Wukong frowned and turned his head.
"What are you, you?"
The Heewon he knew. His family, who had taught him human life for the past eight years.
That family was pointing a knife at him.
"If you don't put him down by the count of three, I'll cut you. One."
Heewon's voice, and Heewon's face. But the moment he sensed the shimmering stories in Heewon's eyes, Great Sage, Heaven's Equal realized.
This isn't Heewon.
"Look at this."
Wukong's muscles twitched all over his body.
"This is starting to piss me off. What the hell are you guys?"
From the youngest, to Heewon. As unprecedented events unfolded one after another, Wukong's patience was reaching its limit.
From afar, Hyunsung shouted.
"No! You can't fight!"
Wukong and Heewon's eyes met, and in the next moment, their hands moved. Sharp blades met fists.
Whoosh.
A soft vibration echoed. It was a familiar vibration. But it stopped Wukong in his tracks.
Because it was a vibration that should never resonate.
In an instant, Wukong, who had distanced himself from the youngest, dug into his arms and pulled out his phone.
<&>「The youngest's smartphone was ringing.」
It shouldn't have been ringing. That's because the phone was always on 'airplane mode'.
—Caller ID blocked.
Great Sage, Heaven's Equal answered. Soon, someone's voice came over the line.

A feeling of peace and comfort. When I opened my eyes, I was in a cozy bed. I couldn't remember how long it had been since I'd woken up. Staring at the unrealistic white ceiling, I blinked absentmindedly.
I wondered if everything had been a dream. But that couldn't be true.
<&>「"I asked, who are you?"」
A voice suddenly came to mind, and memories of the past came crashing back like a storm.
<&>「I jumped into the 'Missing Bush' with Jung Heewon to find Dansu ahjussi.」
And then I arrived in the world where the 'third Kim Dokja' resided. The last memory that came to mind was me regaining consciousness in this body and pushing Great Sage, Heaven's Equal away from the subway.
"Are you awake?"
The door creaked open, and a tall, blond man appeared.
"Ah."
I unconsciously rubbed my eyes, feeling a sense of unreality.
<&>「Great Sage, Heaven's Equal, Sun Wukong, was there.」
The constellation that integrated all of his stories to become the 'Oldest Liberator'.
One of the four veterans of the early channel, who had bestowed upon Kim Dokja the epithet 'Prisoner of the Golden Headband'.
<&>「These aren't my feelings.」
I knew it. This was my first time seeing Great Sage, Heaven's Equal. Nevertheless, a part of my heart throbbed.
<&>「He chose this worldline and has always protected Kim Dokja.」
As a fragment of Kim Dokja, I could understand. How much the third Kim Dokja relied on and adored him, how long he had been a part of Kim Dokja's family.
<&>「Kim Dokja wasn't an only child in this world.」
Amidst a whirlwind of longing, envy, and an inexplicable sense of fulfillment, I looked at Great Sage, Heaven's Equal, unsure of what to say.
Great Sage, Heaven's Equal, looking at me, scratched his cheek with an embarrassed expression and asked,
"You're the 'youngest' from another world!"
"Oh, yes. Let me introduce myself first. I—"
I was momentarily speechless. Who should I say I am?
Great Sage, Heaven's Equal chuckled at my appearance and said,
"Kim Dokja, of course."
"Yes."
That's right. I am 'Kim Dokja' now. But I wasn't the Kim Dokja Great Sage, Heaven's Equal knew. Oddly enough, that fact stung a corner of my heart.
"I'm sorry. There was a misunderstanding. I heard the general story from this guy."
Great Sage, Heaven's Equal was clutching a smartphone in his hand. Great Sage, Heaven's Equal and a smartphone? It seemed like an odd combination, but upon closer inspection, I saw someone's name written on the screen.
<&>「Demon King of Salvation.」
It seemed the 'Demon King of Salvation' had called me while I was asleep.
"Dokja-ssi! Are you awake?"
A thud sounded from behind the door, and Jung Heewon peeked her face out. She pushed Great Sage, Heaven's Equal aside, entered the room, and grabbed my hand.
"Are you hurt? What the hell is this, really?"
I stared at Jung Heewon quietly. The scars on her face and her attire were strangely different from the Jung Heewon I knew in the 41st round.
But the story I felt inside her was definitely the 'Light Sword Emperor Jung Heewon' I knew.
"Heewon-ssi, did you really enter that body?"
"Yes, when I woke up, this was the state I was in."
Jung Heewon clenched and unclenched her fists repeatedly, her expression disbelieving.
"She really is a strong body. Unbelievably."
I suppose so. Jung Heewon in the 1,864th round was the pinnacle of Jung Heewon's abilities.
"It's a shame I can't use stories or her skills. I'd like to try it out."
I smiled bitterly at Jung Heewon.
The moment I possessed this worldline, I immediately realized what kind of world it was.
In this world, the power of the 'System' cannot be used.
Not stories, not stigma, not skills. The unique powers that the <Star Stream> allowed were strictly controlled here. Just like the 'reality' we knew.
"It's fortunate that we can't use them."
"Huh? Why?"
"Because if that were possible, we would be in real danger."
I vaguely remembered the fight between Asmodeus and the Great Sage, Heaven's Equal just before I passed out.
Even in this world with its limited systems, Asmodeus could plot such a bold scheme. If the Great Sage, Heaven's Equal's body hadn't been abnormally strong, I would have been dragged away by Asmodeus without a fight.
"There are others besides us who know the coordinates of this worldline."
I recalled the information I'd heard from the Recorder of Fear, Hermit, in the 'Records Archives'.
<&>「Emperor. Monkey. Sword and shield.」
He had provided those keywords regarding the worldline related to the third Kim Dokja. His keywords likely referred to this world.
The Emperor and the monkey represented the Great Sage, Heaven's Equal. The sword and shield likely referred to Jung Heewon and Lee Hyunsung.
Hermit had already implied that the coordinates of this place were secretly known. That's right—
"I know that."
Great Sage, Heaven's Equal, who had been listening to my story, nodded.
"You're not the first to come here."
"Not the first time."
I wanted to ask if I knew anyone who had visited this worldline. But my question remained unanswered. Another man appeared at the doorway, a puff of steam rising from his face.
"Are you awake?"
A polite yet profound voice.
I got goosebumps again.
<&>「Lee Hyunsung, the Steel Sword Emperor.」
Lee Hyunsung, the man I'd always admired from <Kim Dokja Company>, was there.
He glanced at Jung Heewon, who was holding my hand with a somewhat dark expression, and placed a steaming bowl in front of me.
"I made some porridge."
"Oh, thank you."
"Eat it while it's still warm."
I hesitantly picked up my spoon. Come to think of it, I was quite hungry.
"Well... excuse me."
I took a small sip.
The hot rice melted in my mouth. It was a delicious, well-seasoned porridge. It wasn't as exquisite as Yoo Joonghyuk's, but it was still a delicate dish, a testament to the chef's dedication.
The three of them watched me silently for a while as I ate the porridge. Great Sage, Heaven's Equal looked impressed, and Jung Heewon seemed somewhat relieved. Lee Hyunsung's face was strangely gloomy, yet his eyes were filled with joy.

"I'd like to ask you a few questions."
"Yes."
"Are you from the worldline chosen by the other comrades?"
As expected, Lee Hyunsung seemed to have already guessed where I came from.
I nodded and answered.
"Yes."
"I see."
Lee Hyunsung asked again, his expression somewhat grave.
"Are they... well?"
For a moment, I didn't know what to say. It seemed Lee Hyunsung didn't know anything about the good from other worlds. Considering the limited system here, this wasn't entirely unusual.
"I don't know either. I've only seen them a few times."
"Is that so?"
Lee Hyunsung's face darkened even further. Seeing him like that was heartbreaking, and I wanted to say something. I wanted to write a single line, even if it was a lie, that could comfort him.
I hesitated for a moment before speaking.
"But those people."
I don't know what happened to the members of <Kim Dokja Company> in the eight years I was gone, their circumstances, what they were going through—I don't know. But even without knowing, there are things I can say.
"They haven't given up."
That's something I knew without even asking.
"They still haven't given up on anything."
Lee Hyunsung slowly raised his head, his eyes strangely red.
He licked his lips several times, as if he wanted to ask me something. But even with his large size, there seemed to be something difficult to ask, and Lee Hyunsung couldn't speak.
After biting his lip for a long time, Lee Hyunsung finally opened his mouth, seemingly determined.
Tsutsutsu.
With a faint spark, Jung Heewon, who had been beside him, collapsed onto the bed.
"Heewon-ssi!"
Lee Hyunsung and I simultaneously checked her pulse. Her saddle was trembling unsteadily. Great Sage, Heaven's Equal, approaching, quickly examined her complexion and said,
"It's a side effect of the 'broken film theory'."
I quickly understood the situation. Right now, Jung Heewon from the 41st and 1,864th rounds coexisted in that body.
"This can't last long."
When two essentially identical souls coexist in a single body, their memory films become entangled.
"Both souls have too strong an ego. If this continues, the body will not be able to withstand the collision and will collapse."
If one soul could naturally merge with the other, that might be a different story, but in this situation, that wasn't the case.
Great Sage, Heaven’s Equal sighed deeply, looked at Jung Heewon, then back at me, and said in a somewhat serious voice,
"You must leave this world immediately."
